Saag Paneer

Description

Saag paneer is a classic Indian dish of cooked spinach studded with cubes of fried paneer cheese. Thickened with cream or coconut milk, it's a hearty and filling vegetarian meal.

Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Spinach
  • Garlic and Ginger
  • Herb, Spices and Seasoning
  • Whipping Cream
  • Tomato

Steps

  1. Bring a large saucepan of water to a boil; add spinach and fenugreek and cook until wilted, about 3 minutes. Drain well.li>
  2. Transfer to a food processor and blend until finely chopped, about 5 pulses.
  3. Heat 1 tablespoon canola oil in a large skillet over medium heat; add paneer cubes and fry until browned on all sides, about 5 minutes. Transfer paneer onto a plate.
  4. Heat remaining 2 tablespoons canola oil in the same skillet over medium heat; add cumin seeds and fry until lightly toasted and aromatic, about 3 minutes.
  5. Add onion; cook and stir until softened, 4 to 5 minutes. Add garlic and ginger and stir to coat.
  6. Stir in tomato, garam masala, turmeric, and cayenne pepper; cook until tomato breaks down, stirring often, about 10 minutes.
  7. Stir in pureed spinach mixture, paneer cubes, and cream; add salt to taste.
  8. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
